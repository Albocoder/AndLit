Project: Plain Glass

About the project

This is the Senior Year Project of our group. This project aims to make smart glasses smarter by adding awesome new features. These features consist of Facial Recognition and Text Recognition. Facial Recognition will bring the futuristic spy movies to your life with the click of a button. While text recognition will be a hands-free mode of operation of the smart glasses, allowing you to hear text rather than read it. The assistant you all have been waiting for is just a few months away from you.

Features

Facial Recognition

With our smart glasses, you will be able to take pictures of people and run our facial recognition algorithm on them to know who is present in the picture. You will have 2 options to know the output of the facial recognition algorithm. You can either look at it in your smartphone, where the information about each person in the picture will be displayed on top of the picture you just took, or you can listen the information about the people in the picture in your earphone

Discover More

Text Recognition

In this mode, the smart glasses will read, in our earphone, any text displayed in the picture. This mode is great for people who have motion sickness but love to read while travelling. All you would have to do is to take a picture of the text you want to read using either the camera in the smart glasses or your smartphone's camera, and our text recognition algorithm will do rest of the magic for you
