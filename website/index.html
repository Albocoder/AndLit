About the project

This is the Senior Project of our group. This project aims to create a smart earpieces by adding awesome new features. These features consist of Facial Recognition and Text Recognition. Facial Recognition will bring the perks of recognising faces like in a spy movie, while voice recognition will be a hands-free mode of operation. The assistant you all have been waiting for is just a few months away from you.

Features

Facial Recognition

With our product, you will be able to take pictures of people and run our facial recognition algorithm on them to know who is present in the picture. The app will display info about the people and read briefly through earpiece.

Text Recognition

In this mode, the earpiece will read, any text displayed in the picture. This mode is great for people who have motion sickness but love to read while traveling. All you would have to do is to take a picture of the text you want to read using the camera in the earpiece, and our text recognition algorithm will do rest of the magic for you.

Voice Command

This will be the hands-free mode. In this case you can trigger the automatic listener to do what you command it to. For example you can ask it to take a picture. Then ask it to show you how many people it knows in the picture. You will be having your own smart assistant answering your questions.

Social media sync

Don't start from zero. Login using your social media and we will construct a database of known people. The earpiece will know who they are at first sight. It will work on with Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn and Twitter.

Cloud service for training classifier

Train your classifier to get higher accuracy on the cloud. Classifier can be transferred very easily on the cloud.

Join a pool

You can share your classifier with a pool of people and get to query their classifier too. Now you can disginguish more faces with the expense of having yours be distinguished as well. Sharing is caring!
